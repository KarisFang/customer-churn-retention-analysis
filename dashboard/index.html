04 — Retention recommendations
📋
Contract Upgrade Campaign
Target month-to-month customers with 12+ months of tenure. They've proven loyalty — offer a discount to lock in an annual plan and cut their churn risk from 47.7% to ~20%.
⏱️
Month-6 Check-in Programme
The 6–11 month window has a 60.9% churn rate. A proactive outreach at Month 5 — satisfaction call, usage tips, or a loyalty bonus — can dramatically reduce early-life churn.
🛡️
Free Tech Support Trial
Fiber optic customers without tech support churn at the highest rate. Offering a 3-month free trial of tech support to this segment addresses both service quality and stickiness.
